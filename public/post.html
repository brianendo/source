Introducing Source: Bringing the local community together



Source is supposed to be a place that represents people. We are focusing on the idea of building a community and crowd sourcing our content. We believe that traditional media is putting too much focus on the national concerns so they disregard the day to day life of everyday people. Source is going to provide the news that matters to the people. We are an open community so we will begin recruiting writers on a per need basis as we try to grow this community.
